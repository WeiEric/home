ICS Pygame Project

My Intro to CS class required me to make a non-violent game with certain requirements, so I decided to make a game where the player is a red ball, trying to dodge white balls that bounce off invisible walls until you get hit. Most of the actual code was written at the last minute, but I had the idea for quite a while. I tried to find a partner for this project, but had no luck, and therefore did it alone, and although the mark wasn't good, and I didn't follow some of the requirements, I'm proud of what I came up with in a short amount of time.
